Svartur Full Size Hot Glue Gun

The Svartur Full Size Hot Glue Gun is a versatile tool for both hobbyists and professionals. With dual power settings (60W/100W) and an OFF switch, you can easily adjust the temperature to suit your project. This makes it the ideal dual power glue gun for precision and control.
