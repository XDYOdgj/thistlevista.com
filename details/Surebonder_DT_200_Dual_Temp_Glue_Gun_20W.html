Surebonder DT-200 Dual Temperature Glue Gun

The Surebonder DT-200 Dual Temperature Glue Gun is an essential tool for crafters and DIY enthusiasts. With its 20-watt power, it provides steady and efficient heat for precise bonding. This versatile glue gun features Hi/Lo temperature settings that allow you to choose the right heat level for delicate or heavy-duty projects, ensuring strong results without damaging your materials. Its compact design is ideal for both small and large crafting tasks, making it a convenient addition to your workspace. The trigger-fed system offers precise control over glue application, delivering a professional finish each time.

Perfect for All Your Creative Needs

Whether you're working on fabric art, ceramics, collages, or other creative projects, the DT-200 is built to deliver consistent performance. The dual temperature functionality makes it highly adaptable, allowing you to bond materials of different sensitivities with confidence. The included metal flip stand ensures stability and ease of use, while the adjustable glue stick length options provide flexibility across various project setups. This craft glue gun is safe, reliable, and designed for maximum efficiency, making it a top choice for serious crafters.
